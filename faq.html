Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

What is Premium and how do I upgrade?

Premium unlocks exclusive internships, priority application reviews, personalized mentor guidance, and early notifications. Click 'Upgrade Now' on the Premium page to subscribe.

How can I apply for internships?

Browse available internships on your dashboard. Click 'Apply' on the listings that interest you and fill out the application form.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is never shared with third parties without consent.

Who can I contact for support?

You can reach our support team via the 'Contact Us' page or by emailing support@internquest.com.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription anytime from your account settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

How often are new internships added?

New internship opportunities are added weekly. Make sure to check your dashboard regularly or enable notifications for instant updates.

Do I get notifications for internship deadlines?

Yes, Premium users receive early notifications for upcoming deadlines. Free users can view deadlines on the dashboard but may not get early alerts.

Can I edit my application after submission?

Once submitted, applications cannot be edited. You can withdraw and resubmit a new application if necessary.

Is there a mobile app for Intern Quest?

Currently, Intern Quest is fully optimized for mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future releases.
